# combat


## on attack

1. attacker calculates:
    1. self.color vs land.color bonus
        * +n same color
        * +0 neighbor colors
        * -n opposite colors
    2. weapon advantage
        * magic
        * ranged
        * meelee
    3. situation advantage
        * high ground
        * standing
        * prone
2. defense calculates:
    1. self.color vs land.color bonus
    2. defense status
        * surprised
        * default
        * braced
