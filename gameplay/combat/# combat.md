# combat


## attacking and defending
_does not change with attack choice_

* +1d4 on self color
* -+0  on next colors
* -1d4 on opposite colors

## advantage system
_these are optional, use is decided at beginning of battle_

* weapon
    * advantage
    * balanced
    * disadvantage

* situation
    * standing _vs_ prone
    * high ground _vs_ standing
